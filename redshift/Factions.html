Redshift: Factions

Factions of Redshift

The Mekrai

Sentient robots always with a resembling feature, their emoticon faces which are displayed on their low-resolution face monitors. Unlike many factions, Mekrai are almost fully interchangeable as they split into different “modules” which can be put together to suit the Commander's need in battle.

Subfactions

  • Steam: Grants increased movement speed to all units.
  • Vise: Enhances melee attack strength and durability.
  • Kin: Improves unit coordination and shared abilities.
